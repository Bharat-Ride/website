Welcome to BharatRide! With our app, you can easily manage all your interactions with Parents including collecting monthly fess and keep track of the payment history as well as share the location of your vehicle with parents in real-time, ensuring children’s safety and peace of mind for their parents. BharatRide app is designed to make the process of getting children to and from school as smooth and stress-free as possible. With BharatRide, you can share the real-time location of your school van or rickshaw with parents, ensuring that they always know where you are and when you will reach. Parents will receive notifications when your vehicle is approaching the pickup and drop-off locations. No more waiting for the children - simply start the trip in the app and parents will know exactly when to expect the van. Save time and fuels with reduced wait time. We understand the importance of keeping children safe while they're on the way to and from school. That's why we've created this app to give you the power to share the visibility into children’s transportation with their parents. With BharatRide, you can avoid getting calls from parents to know about your location while your are on your waymaking the ride safer for your and the children. In addition to van tracking, BharatRide also allows you to easily collect and manage monthly payments from the parents for van services. With a few clicks, you can view your payment history, receive a payment, or update your billing information. This saves you time and hassle, as you no longer have to worry about keeping track of payments from the parents or manually following up for the payments. Overall, BharatRide app is the ultimate tool for drivers who want to stay connected with parents for their children's transportation and easily manage payments. We are committed to providing a reliable, convenient, and stress-free experience for both parents and van drivers. Give our app a try and see the difference it can make.
