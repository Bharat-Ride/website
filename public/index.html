General Information

  • 01
    What is BharatRide?

    BharatRide is a mobile application that allows parents to track their children's journey to and from school in real-time, pay digitally and even allows to inform the drivers about the leave in-app, itself.

  • 02
    How does BharatRide work?

    Once you download the app, you can register yourself and provide your driver details to us. Once they onboard you’ll be able to track the vehicle in real-time and receive alerts for pre-post pickup/dropoff, digital payments, leave information, accidental alerts, and assistance.

  • 03
    Is BharatRide safe to use? What happens if there is an emergency during transit?

    Yes, BharatRide is completely safe to use and we take data privacy and security very seriously, and all data is encrypted and stored securely. In case of an emergency, BharatRide will play a pivotal role to provide on-time assistance. We’ll alert you as well as we’ll dispatch drivers near the location to help until our emergency team arrives.

Features

  • 01
    What kind of alerts does BharatRide offer?

    BharatRide offers pre-post pickup/dropoff alerts, payment due alerts, overspeeding alerts, and emergency alerts.

  • 02
    What kind of reports can I get with BharatRide?

    BharatRide offers standard reports that give you real-time insights into your child's journey. We’ll even provide payment reports and invoices.

  • 03
    Can I track multiple children with BharatRide?

    Yes, you can register multiple children and track them all in the app. Once the Ride starts, certainly two profiles with Child names will be visible to swap and track.

Subscription and Technical

  • 01
    Is there a cost to use BharatRide?

    BharatRide offers a free trial period, after which you can choose to subscribe to a monthly or annual plan.

  • 02
    What happens if I cancel my subscription to BharatRide?

    You will lose access to the app's features, such as real-time tracking and alerts if you cancel your subscription. And even post that month you’ll have to rely on drivers for each problem we solve.

  • 03
    Will BharatRide work in all areas?

    BharatRide relies on GPS and cellular data, so as long as there is a network connection in the area, the app should work. However, in areas with poor network coverage, the app's performance may be affected.
